What if I can't stay at a recommended hotel?

No worries at all — we simply recommend staying somewhere around Tequesquitengo so you remain close to the shuttle routes.

  • Ask your hotel to arrange transport to one of the shuttle hotels
  • We strongly recommend avoiding taxis directly to the venue

Weather at the Venue

Even though it's December, Morelos stays warm and sunny during the day.

  • Daytime temperatures can reach around 30°C
  • Evenings cool down to around 12°C

Light clothing during the day, a jacket or shawl for the evening.

Can I bring children?

We love your little ones — please reach out to us directly so we can discuss arrangements and make sure everyone is comfortable throughout the weekend.
